# Portability information report

**Portability information report**

**What is the portability information report?**

The portability information report allows the service provider to retrieve the status of each of their subscribers’ portability requests.  
By using this report, the service provider can maintain their systems up to date with all the information regarding lines due to port-out or port-in and this information is useful in market analysis to identify their position in the market
