Is the user experiencing an issue with an eSIM?

Unlike physical SIM cards, cross-testing is not applicable to eSIMs.  
Troubleshooting must therefore focus on verifying the eSIM profile, both on the device and on the **Auriga** platform.

### Checks to perform

- The eSIM profile has been successfully downloaded onto the device
- The eSIM profile was installed without errors
- The profile is activated and selected as the primary line on the device
- The eSIM is active in Auriga (status **“Active”**, not suspended or terminated)

These checks can be performed:

- **On the device**: via SIM / network settings
- **On Auriga**: by checking the line status
