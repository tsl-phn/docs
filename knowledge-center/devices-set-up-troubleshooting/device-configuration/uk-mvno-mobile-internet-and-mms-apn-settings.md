**UK MVNO mobile internet and MMS APN settings** 

Transatel’s UK MVNOs use EE’s consumer APN to access data.   
  
This means that the APN is normally configured automatically on most devices.  
  
If this is not the case, please go to Settings and Mobile Networks on your phone and enter the following APN settings:   
  
![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-1.png)
  
Remember to check the status of the SIM card to be sure that it is activated and ready to be used.
