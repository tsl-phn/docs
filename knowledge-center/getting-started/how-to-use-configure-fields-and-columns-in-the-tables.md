---
title: "Configuring fields and columns in the tables"
---

Each table in the interface can be exported in csv format – just use the download icon   
  
![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-1.png)
All the columns can be customized with the fields that are important to you, in the order that you wish (the preferences are saved in your browser’s cookies, so you will not find them if you use another computer or browser)  
To configure the columns, click on the + icon, check the fields you need and drag and drop them in the order you want:

![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-2.gif)
