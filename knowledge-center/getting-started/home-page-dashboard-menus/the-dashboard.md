We are in the process of upgrading our dashboard page.

In the coming releases this year you will see added:

- monthly usage of your fleet
- SIM order follow-up, alerts…

The current view version is the following:

You have the choice between

- the graph view (default when you log in)
- the table view

To switch between, view, use this little toggle button at the top right

![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-1.png)

## The Graph view

You can see up to 6 accounts in graph view. If you have access to more than 6 different accounts, the top 6 (in number of lines) will be displayed in graph view.

![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-2.png)

Once you have selected a single account, only this account will appear in the dashboard

![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-3.png)

## SIM Inventory graph

The donut chart shows the account’s lines by status.

you can:

- see the number of lines by hovering your cursor

![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-4.png)

- click on a status to display the lines in this status
- click on “see more” to display all the lines (this is a shortcut to the “All Asset” menu)

![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-5.png)

## Group graph

The histogram on the right shows your groups.

You can

- see the number of SIMs in each status by hovering your cursor

![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-6.png)

- click on the name of a group to display all the SIMs in this group

![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-7.png)

- click on “See more” to see all the groups (this is a shortcut to the Group menu)

![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-5.png)

## Table view

Once you have switched to table view, 2 tables will appear:

- a table summarizing the status of your SIMs
- a table below listing all the groups of your account(s)

Click on any number to access the related SIMs.

![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-8.png)
