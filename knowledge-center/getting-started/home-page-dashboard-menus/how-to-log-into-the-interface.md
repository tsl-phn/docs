**Logging in the interface**

Once your user account has been created by your account administrator, you will receive an email to finalize the account creation and choose your password.  
  
This is the email you should have received  
![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-1.gif)
  
You can now click on the link in the text, and choose your password.  
  
  
**Your first log in**  
Please note that our platform uses user logins that are not necessarily your email.  
The user login is reminded in the email, so make sure to use it when accessing the platform, or to save your password in your browser.  
![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-2.png)
