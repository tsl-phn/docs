**Sw-API: providing custom switching information**

**What is Sw-API**

Some MVNOs may wish to provide their own text in case of switching information requested by a subscriber, or in the case of a switch. To do this, they must build and expose the Switching API (Sw-API) for Transatel to consume and retrieve the custom switching information.  
More information and other opitons for Switching Information click [here](https://docs.transatel.com/uncategorized/switching-information/).
