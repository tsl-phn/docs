**SIM page & checkup panel overview**

This is how a SIM’s page looks like in the interface:   
![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-1.gif)
  
on the left side, you have the 5 tabs to access the SIM details, manage plans, configure services, check usage and logs  
  
![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-2.gif)
  
On the right side at the top, you have **3 actions buttons**  
– change status (the choices will depend on your SIM’s current status)  
– PIN/PUK display  
– Other actions: a shortcut to many actions such as changing Service Profiles, Groups…  
  
![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-3.gif)
  
  
Last but not least, the right side is occupied by the checkup panel, which displays the latest details :  
  
![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-4.png)
  
It includes :   
– the **SIM’s current status** & **[last network attach](network-attach-diagnostic.md) information**   
  
![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-5.png)
  
– the l**ast communications,** including if there is an [**ongoing data session**](https://docs.transatel.com/sim-management/data-session-diagnostic-tool/)  
  
![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-6.png)
