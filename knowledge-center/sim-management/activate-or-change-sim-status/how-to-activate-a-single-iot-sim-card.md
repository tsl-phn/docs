**Activating a single IoT SIM card**

This article explains how to activate a SIM card with our **IoT** offers.  
**Our MVNO customers (with offers requiring portability) should refer to the article relevant to their offer.**  
  
To activate multiple SIM cards, please read this article on [bulk activations](how-to-activate-sim-cards-in-bulk.md).   
  
To be activated, a SIM card must be in available or preactivated status.   
  
To activate a single SIM card, follow these steps :  
  
1. Go to the SIM card’s page and click on the **Activate** button at the top right of the screen  
  
![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-1.gif)
  
2. Select the **[Service Profile](https://docs.transatel.com/services-setup/what-is-a-service-profile/) to apply to the SIM**   
  
![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-2.gif)
  
3 & 4. (Optional Steps) Add a [**reference**, **line & subscriber details**](../how-to-group-tag-and-label-your-sims.md), and add the SIM to a **group**  
The next steps are optional – at this point you can already activate the SIM.  
But if you wish, you can go through the last steps, where you can enter a reference, add the SIM to a group, and add line details.  
  
![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-3.gif)
  
You can now launch the activation order.  
SIM card activations are processed extremely quickly for IoT customers.  
  
![image](/knowledge-center/assets/image-4.png)
